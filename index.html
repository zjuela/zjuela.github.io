About Me

Who Am I?

I'm Zhe Hu. I am working at Apple now.

Before joining Apple, I worked at Hikvision and Light for a few years.

I received my Phd in Computer Science at University of California, Merced, working with Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. My research interests include computer vision, computational photography and machine learning.
